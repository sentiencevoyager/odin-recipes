Dinuguan Recipe

Description

Dinuguan is a savoury dish made with diced pork, pork blood and spices. This classic Filipino pork stew is hearty, boldly flavoured, and delicious as a main meal with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz pork blood
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 1 onion (peeled and chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (peeled and minced)
  • 1 ginger (thumb-sized)
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (patis)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 bsp brown sugar
  • 2 chilies (siling haba)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine pig's blood and 2 tbsp of the vinegar and sti r well
  2. In a pot, over medium heat, heat oil and add onions, garlic, and ginger and cook until softened
  3. Add pork and cook, stirring occasionally until lightly browned
  4. Add fish sauce and cook for about 1 to 2 minutes
  5. Add vinegar and bring to a boil, cook until slightly reduced
  6. Add water and bring to a boil, lower heat, cover, and continue to cook for about 15 to 20 minutes until the meat is tender
  7. Add pork blood, stir out the lumps
  8. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve
  9. Add chili peppers
  10. Continue to simmer for about 10 minutes until sauce is thickened
  11. Season with salt and pepper to taste, serve and enjoy!
